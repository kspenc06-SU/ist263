Best Restaurants in Nashville!

The most famous food that Nashville has to offer is hot chicken, and there is no place that offers it like Hattie B’s. Hattie B’s has locations all over Tennessee and some in other states such as Georgia, Texas, and Alabama. They offer six different spices levels, from “Southern” to “Shut the Cluck Up!”, so everyone is able to find the spice level that works for them. Hattie B’s also offers an assortment of sides like southern greens and mac & cheese, and desserts like banana pudding and peach cobbler.

Another type of food that Nashville offers is southern BBQ, and Edley’s is the perfect place to find it. They have locations all over the Nashville area and are known for their wide variety of food options. They offer different types of meat such as pork, chicken, and brisket. Edley’s also has a wide range of sides, from potato salad to jalapeno cornbread. It is very easy to get to-go containers of your favorite sides to bring home to enjoy while watching the big game or eating with family!

Nashville is mostly known for Broadway, which is the street filled with “Honky Tonks” owned by famous country stars from Jason Aldean to Blake Shelton. They offer southern comfort food, and when it gets later in the night, they become very popular spots to see different music performers. As you walk through the street, you can hear all of the different singers in their respective locations, and it is also a great way to try all of the food and drinks that each of these places offer. Some of these venues include Tootsie’s, Legends Corner, and Dierk Bentley’s Whiskey Row.
