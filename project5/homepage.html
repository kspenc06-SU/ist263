Welcome to Nashville!

This website is about all things Nashville! From facts about the city, to fun things to do and food to eat, this site has everything you need! Facts about Tennessee:

  • It is known as “The Volunteer State”
  • The capital city is Nashville (pop: 1.3 million)
  • The largest city is Memphis (pop: 628,000)
  • The state has a population of 6.9 million
  • The state bird is the mockingbird
  • The state flower is the iris
Facts about Nashville:
  • The city was named after Revolutionary War hero Francis Nash
  • It was founded on December 24th, 1779
  • The first FM broadcast license was given to Nashville WSM station in 1941
  • The first service dog for the blind was brought back to the US from overseas to Nashville
  • The Grand Ole Opry is the world’s longest-running live music show was hosted in Nashville
  • The city is home to the largest festival for songwriters called “Tin Pan South” where hundreds of writers come to perform their original work
